SABnzbd Client Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 31, 2025

Overview

SABnzbd Client is a remote control app for SABnzbd servers. Your privacy is important to us.

Data Collection

This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to us. All data stays on your device or is transmitted directly to your own SABnzbd server.

Server Connection

The app connects directly to SABnzbd servers that you configure. Server addresses and API keys are stored locally on your device and are never sent to any third party.

Analytics

This app does not use any analytics or tracking services.

Advertising

This app may display ads provided by Google AdMob. Google may collect data as described in their privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
